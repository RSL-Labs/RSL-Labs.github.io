The Eye of the Storm


Sprawling castles built of steel

reaching to the clouds.

From the tower of our profits,

ignoring what is real.

(what is real!)


Engine of mankind's destruction,

fueled by all our sins.

Pounding hammer of invention,

driving last nails in.

(last nails in!)
