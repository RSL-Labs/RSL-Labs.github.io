Secrets of the Ancients


The Pyramids of Giza old,

in Ra's light, shining gold.

Secrets of the pharaohs kept;

within the tombs- where they slept.


Through the gate of Ishtar looking on,

under azure skies of Babylon,

The hanging gardens verdant green,

among the gates of lapis lazuli.


CHORUS:

Our elders spoke the language of the stars~

in tongues forgotten and now lost.

Ancient secrets brought from afar~

cosmic wisdoms of the greatest cost.


Colossus of great Helios,

astride the bay of Rodes.

The guiding light of Pharos~,

beacon of Alexandria.


In the glow of the full moon:

your temple stands. Oh Artemis!

Of the love men showed you then-

a single ruined pillar stands.


[CHORUS]


A temple built to father Zeus,

there at the foot of Mount Olympus.

King of Caria, oh great Mausolus.

Of all you built: only name remains.
